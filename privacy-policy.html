Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Modam, Inc. ("we," "us") collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use the Modam platform.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect data necessary to provide and secure our marketplace services.

  • Account Data: Profile name, email address, and authentication identifiers provided by third-party logins (like Apple or Google).
  • Marketplace Data: Images you upload, item descriptions, messages sent to other users, and community forum posts.
  • Transaction Data: Shipping addresses and order information. Modam does not process or store full credit card numbers. All payment processing is handled by our secure partner, Stripe.
  • Technical Data: Standard server logs, device identifiers, app version, and basic usage analytics used exclusively to maintain and improve platform security and reliability.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process your data to:

  • Operate the marketplace and facilitate transactions between artists and buyers.
  • Communicate with you regarding your account, orders, or support requests.
  • Prevent fraud, enforce our Terms of Service, and maintain trust and safety.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with essential third-party service providers acting on our behalf (e.g., Supabase for secure database hosting) and independent processors acting as data controllers for specific functions (e.g., Stripe for payouts and payment processing). We may also disclose data if legally required by valid law enforcement requests.

4. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed on servers located outside of your home country. We utilize reputable infrastructure providers and ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to secure your data internationally.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data. You can manage your profile directly in the app. For comprehensive data removal, please refer to our Data Deletion Policy.

6. Security and Children's Privacy

We employ industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) and modern access controls to protect your data. However, no digital transmission is entirely secure. Furthermore, Modam is not intended for use by children under 14; we do not knowingly collect their data.

7. Contact

For privacy inquiries or to exercise your data rights, please contact us at modamceo2026@gmail.com.

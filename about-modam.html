About Modam

Modam comes from the Korean words meaning to gather and to hold. We built this platform to make the art world less distant and more transparent.

Mission

Art for everyday life

We empower emerging and established artists to build their business, manage their portfolio, and sell directly to a global audience with simple, straightforward business tools.

For collectors—whether you are buying your first piece or expanding a curated collection—we offer a secure environment to find artworks that move you and keep them close.

A space built around the process of collecting what you love—and shaping it, piece by piece, into your own.

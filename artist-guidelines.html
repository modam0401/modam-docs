Artist Guidelines

Last updated: April 2026

Welcome to Modam! These guidelines support a fair, safe, and creative community for artists and collectors. By selling on Modam, you agree to follow the Rules below. Tips are suggestions only — they are not contractual obligations unless the same requirement appears in the Terms of Service.

Rules = requirements Modam may enforce (warnings, removal, suspension, or termination). Tips = optional ideas for presentation and growth; we do not treat them as binding seller duties.

Rules — artwork listings

Authenticity & Originality

Do

  • Upload only YOUR original work
  • Create all elements yourself
  • Disclose AI assistance if used
  • Own full rights to the work

Don't

  • Copy other artists' work
  • Use copyrighted images
  • Claim AI art as handmade
  • Steal or plagiarize

Secondary Market & Resale

Resale is Allowed. Collectors may freely resell artworks they own. Professional resellers are welcome. Active secondary market increases artwork value. Honest disclosure of resale status is encouraged.

Prohibited Resale Practices: Selling counterfeit copies, coordinated price manipulation, buying from yourself to inflate market, selling stolen artwork, or misrepresenting provenance.

Photo Quality Standards

High-quality photos sell better. Minimum 1000×1000 pixels (1500×1500 recommended). Well-lit, sharp images with accurate colors. Multiple views for 3D work and sculptures. Show scale with room shots or measurements.

Prohibited in Photos: Blurry or pixelated images, heavy filters that distort colors, watermarks covering the artwork, photos of screens.

Accurate Descriptions

Be Honest

  • True dimensions and materials
  • Disclose all imperfections
  • Clear about what's included
  • Honest about artwork condition

Don't Mislead

  • Exaggerate size or quality
  • Hide damage or flaws
  • Use deceptive pricing
  • Fake scarcity tactics

Rules — prohibited content

The following content will result in immediate removal and possible account suspension:

  • Illegal Content: Anything violating local or international law
  • Child Exploitation: Any content involving minors inappropriately
  • Violence/Gore: Extremely graphic violent imagery
  • Hate Symbols: Nazi imagery, hate group symbols, discriminatory content
  • Pornography: Explicit sexual content (artistic nudity may be allowed with proper tagging)

Requires Careful Handling

  • Artistic Nudity: Tag appropriately, must be tasteful and artistic
  • Political Art: Allowed but must not incite violence or hatred
  • Religious Content: Respectful treatment of all religions

Rules — transaction conduct

Communication Standards

  • Response time: Reply to buyer inquiries promptly — within 24 hours when possible for pre-sale questions
  • Professional tone: Courteous, helpful, and clear
  • Transparency: Be upfront about shipping times and policies

Contact information & off-platform diversion

  • Before a valid Modam checkout: Do not solicit buyers to pay outside the app, and do not share personal contact details (phone, personal email, messenger handles, payment IDs, etc.) primarily to move the transaction off Modam or to evade platform checkout.
  • After a valid paid order exists: You may exchange logistics contact details that are reasonably necessary for fulfillment (e.g. phone for courier, localized address clarifications), preferably through Modam chat when possible. Keep such use limited to completing that order.

Prohibited: Harassment, threats, or abuse; asking for payment outside Modam checkout; using Modam chat mainly to divert sales or fees off-platform; spam or unsolicited advertising unrelated to a genuine buyer inquiry.

Shipping Excellence

  • Confirm & ship: After payment, confirm the order, then ship within 5 business days (or as agreed with the buyer)
  • Pack Professionally to prevent damage
  • Carrier & tracking in Modam: In your sale order detail, enter the courier name and tracking number once you have shipped — buyers rely on this to track delivery
  • Communicate Delays immediately if any issues arise

Rules — community participation

Modam's community forum is a space for artists and collectors to share ideas, showcase work, and discuss topics across categories: Art, Daily Life, Finance, Showcase, Free Topic, and Issue Discussion.

Community content rules

Do

  • Use relevant categories for posts
  • Keep discussions respectful
  • Credit others when referencing their work
  • Report inappropriate content

Don't

  • Spam or post repetitive content
  • Harass or demean other users
  • Post misleading or false information
  • Solicit off-platform transactions

Violations in the community are subject to the same consequence system as marketplace violations — repeated offenses can result in account suspension.

Rules — violation consequences

1st Violation — 7-day listing suspension 2nd Violation — 30-day account suspension

3rd Violation or Serious Offense: Permanent account termination. No appeal for serious violations. Payment hold up to 90 days for existing orders.

Immediate ban offenses

  • Fraud or scam attempts
  • Selling stolen artwork
  • Harassment or threats
  • Illegal content
  • Repeated copyright infringement
  • Fake reviews or rating manipulation

Tips — growth and presentation (non-binding)

The following sections are recommendations. They do not create enforceable duties or guarantees about visibility, ranking, or sales.

Build a strong profile

  • Complete Bio: Tell your artistic story (150–300 words)
  • Professional Photo: Clear headshot or studio photo
  • Artist Statement: Share your inspiration and process
  • Regular Updates: Keep profile current with latest work

Visibility, community, and buyers

  • Post regularly: Steady new listings can help collectors discover your work.
  • Join challenges: Participate when available to connect with the community.
  • Forum ideas: Share process or WIP posts, join Issue Discussion / Free Topic threads, use Showcase and link to listings — all optional.
  • Engage: Comment and support others in a genuine way.
  • Respond to buyers: Timely, clear replies often improve trust and completion of sales. Modam does not promise any particular search placement, ranking, or algorithmic boost from response speed.

Optional fulfillment touches

  • Some sellers add a thank-you note or certificate — entirely optional.

Pricing ideas

  • Start Competitive: Research similar artists' pricing
  • Build Gradually: Raise prices as your following grows
  • Limited Editions command higher prices
  • Offer Range: Both affordable and premium pieces

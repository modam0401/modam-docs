Data Deletion Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At Modam, we respect your right to control your personal data. This page explains how to delete your data, what happens when you delete your account, and what data may be retained.

1. Your Right to Delete Data

You have the right to delete your Modam account and associated data at any time, for any reason. The deletion process is:

  • Simple — Can be done directly in the app in just a few taps
  • Immediate — Most data is deleted right away
  • Permanent — Deleted data cannot be recovered
  • Free — No fees or charges for account deletion

2. How to Delete Your Account

Method 1: Delete Through the App

1
Open the Modam app
2
Go to Profile tab
3
Tap the Settings icon in the top right
4
Scroll down to Account Management
5
Tap "Delete Account" and confirm

Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your artworks, messages, and account data will be deleted.

Method 2: Request via Email

Send to: modamceo2026@gmail.com
Subject: Account Deletion Request
Include: Your registered email address, display name (if you remember it)
Response Time: Within 48 hours

4. What Happens After Deletion

  • Your profile disappears from search results immediately
  • Your artworks are removed from the marketplace
  • You are logged out of all devices
  • Your email becomes available for new registrations after 30 days

Cannot Be Recovered: Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered. You will need to create a new account and re-upload your artworks.

5. Partial Data Deletion

If you don't want to delete your entire account, you can remove specific content:

  • Delete an artwork: My Artworks → tap artwork → ⋮ menu → Delete Artwork
  • Clear messages: Messages → long press conversation → Delete Conversation
  • Delete community post: Open your post → tap edit icon → Delete
  • Update profile: Profile → Edit Profile → remove or update any information

6. Legal Data Retention

Some data may be retained for legal or security reasons after account deletion:

  • Financial Records (7 years): Transaction records anonymized and stored for tax compliance
  • Security Logs (90 days): Login IP addresses and fraud prevention data
  • Ban Records: If your account was banned, records are kept to prevent re-registration

7. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

  • Right to be Forgotten — Request complete data deletion
  • Right to Access — Download all your data
  • Right to Rectification — Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Data Portability — Export your data
  • Right to Opt-Out — We don't sell your data

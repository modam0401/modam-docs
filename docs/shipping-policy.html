Shipping Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Modam connects artists and collectors worldwide. Each seller is responsible for shipping their artworks safely and promptly. This policy outlines shipping responsibilities and expectations.

Seller Responsibilities

  • Confirm the order: After the buyer's payment succeeds, review the order in the Modam app before you ship
  • Pack Securely: Bubble wrap or foam for protection, rigid cardboard for large pieces, waterproof outer packaging, and clear "FRAGILE" labels
  • Ship Promptly: Ship within 5 business days of payment confirmation (or as agreed with the buyer in chat)
  • Carrier & tracking in the app: After you ship, open the sale order in Modam and enter the courier (carrier) name and tracking number so the buyer can track the package. Enter this as soon as practical after handoff to the carrier
  • Insurance: Strongly recommended for items over $500
  • Communication: Keep buyer informed of any delays

Shipping Timeframes

Shipping TypeEstimated Delivery
Domestic (Same Country)3–7 business days
International (Express)7–14 business days
International (Standard)14–21 business days

Note: Customs clearance and local delivery may add additional time for international shipments.

Shipping Costs

Seller Responsibility: Sellers bear all shipping costs and must arrange shipment themselves. Modam does not handle shipping directly.

  • Buyers and sellers can discuss shipping details through in-app chat
  • Sellers must mark items as "Sold Out" after completing a sale
  • Sellers may offer local pickup or direct delivery through chat negotiations

Shipping Cost Guidelines

  • Small prints (under 12"): $10–20 domestic, $20–40 international
  • Medium artworks (12"–24"): $20–50 domestic, $50–100 international
  • Large artworks (24"+): $50–150 domestic, $150–300 international
  • Sculptures: Varies by weight and fragility

Lost or Damaged Packages

Direct Transaction

Buyer and seller resolve directly. Modam is not responsible for shipping issues in direct transactions. We recommend using escrow for valuable items.

Escrow Transaction

Protected by Modam Buyer Protection. Report damage within 48 hours of delivery with photo evidence. Admin reviews within 3–5 business days.

International Shipping

  • Customs Fees: Buyer is responsible for all customs duties and taxes
  • Import Regulations: Check your country's art import rules
  • Customs Delays: Can add 5–14 days to delivery time
  • Documentation: Seller must provide customs declaration with actual sale price — under-declaring is illegal

Packaging Best Practices

For Paintings & Prints

1
Cover artwork face with glassine or acid-free paper
2
Place cardboard on both sides
3
Wrap in bubble wrap (3–4 layers)
4
Place in rigid cardboard mailer or box and fill with packing material

For Framed Works

1
Tape an "X" across glass to prevent shattering
2
Protect corners with cardboard corner guards
3
Wrap entire frame in bubble wrap and use double-walled box
4
Add "FRAGILE - GLASS" labels clearly

Delivery Confirmation

  • Signature Required: Recommended for items over $500
  • Mark as Received: Buyers should confirm receipt in app
  • Inspect Immediately: Check for damage upon delivery
  • Buyer Protection Period: 7 days from delivery to open a dispute (escrow transactions only)

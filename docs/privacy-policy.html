2. Legal bases & consent structure

We do not treat all processing as one “mandatory consent.” Different activities rely on different grounds:

2.1 Contract & pre-contract (necessary to provide the Service)

Processing needed to create and secure your account, operate the marketplace (including checkout, order messages, and fraud checks tied to a purchase), and deliver features you request. If you do not provide required fields (e.g. OAuth identity from your chosen provider, delivery contact fields at checkout), we may be unable to provide those features.

2.2 Legitimate interests

Where allowed by law, we process data for security, abuse prevention, product improvement (e.g. aggregated analytics), and internal reporting, balanced against your rights.

2.3 Consent (separate opt-in)

Where required, we rely on separate consent for:

  • Marketing notifications (e.g. promotional push or email) — you can withdraw consent in device or app settings where available.
  • Non-essential cookies or similar technologies on any web properties we operate, per the notice shown there.
  • Sensitive or special-category data — we avoid collecting it unless lawfully permitted and clearly disclosed.

2.4 Legal obligation

We retain or disclose limited records where required for tax, accounting, or lawful requests by authorities.

3. Categories of data

3.1 Account & authentication (contract)

  • Identifiers from Google, Apple, or Facebook when you choose those sign-in methods (e.g. provider user ID, email where exposed by the provider).
  • Profile elements needed to display your account, such as display name and profile image.

3.2 Optional profile & community fields

The following are optional unless you choose to provide them:

  • School / department or similar academic fields.
  • Extended bio or other narrative profile text beyond a minimal display name.
  • Artwork creation location (city / region / country) when you explicitly add it to a listing — we do not collect precise background location for this purpose.
  • Additional social or portfolio links if the app offers them as optional fields.

3.3 Listings, community content, messaging (contract + legitimate interests)

  • Artwork images, titles, descriptions, and prices you upload.
  • Community posts, comments, likes, and bookmarks.
  • Messages you send through the Service.

3.4 Orders & payments

  • Shipping / contact details you enter at checkout.
  • Transaction identifiers and amounts on our systems. Full card or wallet numbers are processed by Stripe, not stored by Modam.

3.5 Technical & usage data

  • Device/app version, diagnostics, and logs for reliability and security.

We do not require access to your contacts or photo library for core account creation. Images come from uploads you initiate. We do not sell personal data.

4. Overseas transfers & recipients

Our infrastructure and partners may process data outside your country. We describe roles at a high level below; commercial terms are governed by our agreements with each vendor.

4.1 Supabase (hosting & auth infrastructure)

Role: Processor / entrusted service provider operating database and authentication infrastructure on our instructions.
Data may be stored or processed in regions Supabase configures for our project. See Supabase’s documentation and DPA for details.

4.2 Stripe (payments)

Role: Independent payment services provider (separate controller for payment and fraud data it processes). Their privacy policy applies to checkout data: Stripe Privacy Policy.

4.3 Google, Apple, Facebook (OAuth)

Role: Independent identity providers. They process sign-in on their terms when you choose “Sign in with …”. Review their privacy notices for those flows.

4.4 Logistics & communications

Where we use email, push, analytics, or error-reporting vendors, they process data as processors or sub-processors under contract, limited to the service provided.

Where applicable law requires, we rely on appropriate safeguards (e.g. standard contractual clauses) or statutory exceptions for international transfers.

5. Sharing overview (provision vs processing)

  • Processors / entrustment: Supabase and similar vendors process data only on our documented instructions.
  • Independent controllers: Stripe, Google, Apple, and Meta (Facebook) control certain data in their own environments.
  • Legal & safety: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect users and the Service.

6. Retention

We keep data while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward as described in Data Deletion. Backups roll off on a cycle (often up to approximately 90 days). Some records are retained longer where law requires (e.g. tax / commerce records), sometimes in minimized or anonymized form.

7. Security

We use encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and vendor security features. No method is 100% secure; please use a strong device passcode and sign out on shared devices.

8. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, rectify, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing, and to withdraw consent where processing was consent-based.

  • Access / copy: You may request a copy of your personal data by emailing modamceo@modam.app with subject “Data access request.” Modam does not guarantee a self-service “download my data” button in the app at all times; we fulfill requests manually where required by law.
  • Correction: Update profile fields in the app where editing is available, or email us.
  • Deletion: Use in-app account deletion where offered, or email us — see Data Deletion.
  • Marketing opt-out: Disable promotional notifications in app or device settings.

9. Children

The Service is intended for users 14+, consistent with our Terms. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 14. If you believe we have, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

10. Changes

We may update this Policy. Material changes will be notified by reasonable means (e.g. in-app notice or email).

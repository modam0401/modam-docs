Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At Modam, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account on Modam, we collect:

  • Email address — Used for account authentication and important notifications
  • Profile information — Display name, profile photo, bio, school/department (optional)
  • Authentication data — Provided by OAuth services (Google, Apple, Facebook) when you sign in

1.2 Content You Create

  • Artworks you upload (images, titles, descriptions, pricing information)
  • Community posts and comments you write (title, body, category)
  • Messages you send to other users
  • Your likes, bookmarks, and interactions with other users' content

1.3 Location Information (Optional)

When you upload artwork, you may optionally choose to include location information — city, state/province, and country where your artwork was created. Location is only collected if you grant permission and actively choose to add it. It is never collected in the background.

1.4 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • App usage data (features used, time spent in app)
  • Error logs and diagnostic data for app improvement

We do NOT collect: background location, your contacts, photos from your gallery (only images you explicitly upload), or your full payment card or wallet credentials — those are handled by our payment partner Airwallex during checkout.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Provision

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your profile and artworks to other users
  • Enable communication between users (messaging, comments)
  • Process artwork sales and purchases

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze app usage to improve features and user experience
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user behavior

2.3 Safety and Security

  • Prevent spam, fraud, and abuse
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect the rights and safety of our users

3. Information Sharing

3.1 Public Information

The following is visible to other Modam users: your profile (display name, photo, bio), artworks you upload, community posts and comments you write, and likes/bookmarks if you make them public.

3.2 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3.3 Third-Party Service Providers

  • Supabase — Database and authentication services
  • Google, Apple, Facebook — Secure OAuth sign-in
  • Airwallex — Payment processing and checkout. Airwallex processes payment instructions; Modam does not store your full card numbers. Airwallex's privacy policy applies to that step: Airwallex Privacy Policy.

4. Data Security

  • Encryption — All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • Secure Authentication — OAuth tokens are securely stored
  • Access Controls — Only authorized personnel can access user data
  • Regular Audits — We regularly review and update our security practices

5. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts — Data is retained while your account exists
  • Deleted Accounts — Most data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Legal Requirements — Some data may be retained longer if required by law
  • Backup Systems — Data in backups is deleted during regular backup cycles (up to 90 days)

6. Your Rights

  • Access & Download — You can access and download your personal data at any time through app settings
  • Modify Information — Update your profile, artworks, and preferences at any time
  • Delete Your Account — See our Data Deletion Policy for details
  • Opt Out — Unsubscribe from marketing emails or disable push notifications in settings

7. Children's Privacy

Modam is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes through in-app notifications or email.

9. Contact Us

Email: modamceo@modam.app

Data Deletion Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

This page explains how to delete your Modam account, what happens to personal data, and what may be retained for legal or security reasons. It aligns with our Privacy Policy.

1. What “deletion” means here

Deletion is a process, not always a single instant event across every system:

  • Immediately or promptly: We deactivate your account and remove or hide your profile and content from active use of the Service (so other users typically cannot see them).
  • Within approximately thirty (30) days: We delete or irreversibly anonymize personal data in our primary (“live”) databases, except where a specific exception below applies.
  • Backups: Residual copies may persist in encrypted backups until those backups expire on a rolling cycle (often up to about 90 days), after which they are overwritten or purged according to vendor tooling.
  • Legal records: Certain transaction or tax-related data may be kept in a separate, restricted form for statutory retention periods (often up to several years), sometimes anonymized or minimized where permitted.

Messages, comments, and posts: We aim to remove them from the live Service quickly. However, limited technical copies (e.g. caches, logs, security investigations, dispute files, or legal holds) may persist for a period where needed for abuse prevention, dispute resolution, or compliance. Such retention is minimized and not used for marketing.

2. How to request deletion

2.1 In the app

1
Open the Modam app.
2
Go to Profile → Settings.
3
Use Account Management → Delete Account and confirm.

2.2 By email

Send to: modamceo@modam.app
Subject: Account Deletion Request
Include: Registered email and display name if known.
We aim to confirm within a few business days.

4. After deletion

  • You are signed out on devices.
  • Your public profile and marketplace listings should disappear from the app’s normal views promptly after deactivation.
  • Re-registration with the same email may be blocked or delayed for a cooling-off period to prevent abuse.

Irreversible: Once deletion completes, you cannot restore the same account history. You may open a new account if permitted.

5. Partial removal

Without deleting your whole account, you may delete individual artworks, posts, or conversations where the app provides those controls.

6. Third parties

Stripe and other payment providers retain certain payment records under their own policies. Modam cannot delete data inside their systems; see Stripe Privacy Policy.

7. Your rights

Depending on jurisdiction, you may have rights of access, correction, erasure, restriction, objection, and portability. Contact modamceo@modam.app. See also Privacy Policy section on access requests.

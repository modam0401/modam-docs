Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At Modam, we strive to ensure both buyers and sellers have a fair and transparent transaction experience. Our refund policy varies depending on the transaction type.

Payments: Purchases on Modam are processed by Airwallex. Any refund that is approved under this policy is initiated in line with our internal procedures and Airwallex's rules; timing may depend on the original payment method.

Direct Transaction

No platform refunds available. For direct transactions, buyers and sellers must negotiate directly. Modam is not responsible for disputes or refunds. We recommend using escrow for valuable items.

  • All sales are final through the platform
  • Buyer and seller communicate directly for any issues
  • Platform provides messaging but no refund processing

Escrow Transaction

Protected by Modam Buyer Protection. Escrow transactions include platform protection and refund processing.

Eligible Refund Reasons

  • Item Significantly Different: Artwork does not match description or photos
  • Damaged During Shipping: Item arrived damaged — must report within 48 hours
  • Item Not Received: Package lost or never delivered
  • Authenticity Issues: Item is not original as described

Refund Request Process

1
Open Dispute within 7 days of delivery confirmation
2
Provide Evidence — upload clear photos showing the issue
3
Admin Review — our team reviews within 3–5 business days
4
Decision — if approved, full refund including platform fees

Return Shipping

  • Seller's Fault: Seller pays return shipping
  • Buyer's Fault: Buyer pays return shipping
  • Original artwork must be returned in same condition
  • Use insured shipping for valuable items

Non-Refundable Cases

The following are NOT eligible for refunds:

  • Buyer changed their mind after purchase
  • Item accurately matches description and photos
  • Minor color differences due to screen calibration
  • Buyer's remorse or preference changes
  • After 7-day dispute window has passed
  • Buyer damaged the item after delivery

Challenge & Auction Items

All sales are final for auction wins. No refunds for challenge auction items. Review artwork details carefully before bidding — bids are binding commitments.

Refund Timeline

  • Dispute Review: 3–5 business days
  • Refund Processing: 5–10 business days after approval
  • Bank Processing: Additional 3–5 business days

Total time: approximately 11–20 business days from dispute resolution.
